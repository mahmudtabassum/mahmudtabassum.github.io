About me

I'm a fourth year PhD student in ECpE Department at Iowa State University, pursuing my research under the guidance of Dr. Mai Zheng.

Research Interest

My research interest falls into the broad area of storage systems such as file systems, file system utilities, parallel & distributed systems. My current research is focused on configuration related-issues in storage systems. I'm part of the Data Storage Lab (DSL) maintained by Dr. Mai Zheng.
